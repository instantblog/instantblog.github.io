About

InstantBlog allows you to view your Instagram account in a one-page diary/blog format. All you need to do is to replace YourUserName with your Instagram's username in this link: http://instantblog.me/YourUserName. For example:

http://instantblog.me/
{{user.username}}

This is how mine looks like:

http://instantblog.me/mynameisyan

You might want to know ...

- InstantBlog uses are for FREE.

- Private users' accounts are NOT viewable on InstantBlog.

- Feel free to use InstantBlog's link as your personal blog page, and to share it with your friends.

"Thank you for using InstantBlog. I hope you are liking it so far! ^ - ^

I created InstantBlog because I was trying to help my cousin build a simple online 'Baby Journal' for her baby, where she can post pictures and write stories daily, then to share with family and friends who cares. At that time, I just got finished making a "Gallery" for my own website by pulling images from my Instagram account, I then came up with this idea of creating InstantBlog.

I will keep improving and refining InstantBlog when time allows. If you have any questions and suggestions, you are welcome to leave me a message under the Contact section.

Thank you for your support ~ !"
